Citation Typing Ontology Intentions

Page with general info about Citation Typing Ontology (CiTO) intention annotation of citations in Wikidata. Using this annotation you can easily find articles which are cited, for example, because they provide a method used by the citing articles. The CiTO was introduced in 2010.
